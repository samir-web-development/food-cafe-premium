15 Years of Craft

Good Cafe was born in the narrow, aromatic lanes of Old Mumbai in 2010 — a city that never sleeps, and a cafe that never stopped dreaming. What began as a modest 12-seat corner shop near the iconic Bandra railway station quickly became the neighbourhood's favourite refuge from the chaos of city life.

"We wanted a place where the first sip feels like coming home — wherever in the world you are from."

Inspired by the chai culture of Rajasthan, the bold spices of Kerala, and the contemporary café movement sweeping India's metropolitan cities, our founders crafted a menu that bridges generations. Every item on our menu carries a memory — a grandmother's cardamom secret, a street vendor's masala trick, reimagined with artisan precision.

Today, Good Cafe stands as a landmark of culinary craft in Mumbai, with its hand-painted walls, brass fixtures, and the eternal scent of freshly ground coffee that drifts through wooden-frame windows every morning. We are not just a café — we are a chapter in the city's living story.

The Visionaries

Meet Our Founders

Arjun Mehta
Co-Founder & Head of Vision

"I believe food is the most honest form of storytelling. Arjun grew up in a family of restaurateurs in Rajasthan, inheriting a deep reverence for spices and hospitality. After training at the Institute of Hotel Management in Mumbai, he spent five years across cafes in Paris and London before returning home with a singular dream — to build a space that felt unmistakably Indian yet universally welcoming."

Priya Sharma
Co-Founder & Culinary Director

"Every recipe is a love letter to a place or a person." Priya's culinary journey began in her grandmother's kitchen in Lucknow, where she first discovered that the secret to great food lies in patience and love. A graduate of Le Cordon Bleu, she has spent over a decade refining the art of fusion cuisine — blending Indian soul with global technique.

Rohan Iyer
Co-Founder & Master Brewer

"Coffee, for me, is a meditation." Born and raised on a coffee estate in Coorg, Karnataka, Rohan developed an intimate understanding of the bean from the very source. A certified Q Grader and specialty coffee consultant, he has curated Good Cafe's entire coffee programme — sourcing single-origin Indian beans and developing our signature masala blends in-house.
